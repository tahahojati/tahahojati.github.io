about me

I'm a lover of learning and a seeker of truth! I also do software development as my day job. I have a B.S. in Physics from U. of Hawaii. While studying, I realized that I really enjoyed coding and working with electronics, so after graduating I taught myself web development using books and online resources. I am lucky because software and algorithm design are skills that just feel natural to me. Some days I sit down and read a technical book for hours on like it is Harry Potter. I am also lucky because many folks decided to trust my skills when I had no proven track of experience and put me in charge of their application. Thanks to them, I was able to become a better Developer.

These days I am trying to orient myself towards the more data-centeric side of computer science. I am quite interested in topics like AI, Machine Learning, IoT, and big data analysis and am actively educating myself in those fields. At the same time, I am still working on my skills as an application/web developer. I have done a few simple Android apps as personal projects, I am contributing to a Code for America project here in Hawaii and I am the proud webmaster of my church! I am also working on couple other "secret" personal projects at the moment. If I ever complete them, I'll share a link here! haha

When I was 18, I chose to study physics because I wanted to make an impact in the world. I needed to gain an understanding and make a difference (and hopefully some profit!) Today, that is still my primary desire and motivation. It makes me a little restless and inpatient but I also believe that it gives me the ability to always keep the big picture in mind and the courage to take risks and dedicate myself to projects.
